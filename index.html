About us

In operation since 2011, Kilmore Quay Kayak Tours offer their customers a fantastic opportunity to explore one of Irelands hidden gems. A family run business where Tom, the owner, has created a unique kayaking experience for all to enjoy. Tom has over 25 years of kayaking experience and is incredibly knowledgable about the local area. Wheter it's fishing off of St. Patricks bridge or exploring the Saltee Islands, you'll be sure to have an unforgettable experience with Kilmore Quay Kayak Tours.
